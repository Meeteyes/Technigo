SaaS

*Software as a service

Software as a service (or SaaS) is a way of delivering applications over the Internet—as a service. Instead of installing and maintaining software, you simply access it via the Internet, freeing yourself from complex software and hardware management. SaaS is often regfered as the “software on demand”. Software as service popularity is rapidly growing. Some popular examples of SaaS: Google G-Suite, Dropbox, Netflix etc. The most obvious and most-often cited advantage of the SaaS distribution model is the reduction in initial costs from the development, deployment, and maintenance of software applications.
